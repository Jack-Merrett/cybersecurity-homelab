## Test Objective

Determine whether a WireGuard-connected travel router can reliably support remote work from Japan while routing traffic through a homelab in Hawaii.

Success Criteria:

- Access internal homelab resources
- Stable VPN connection
- Successful inbound and outbound work calls
- No significant latency issues
- No noticeable degradation of user experience
